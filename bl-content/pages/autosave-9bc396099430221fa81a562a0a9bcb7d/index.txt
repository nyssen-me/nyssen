<p>I help organisations make their websites, documents, and digital products accessible to everyone. With over 20 years of experience in web design and development, I bring a deep understanding of how things are built, so I can identify barriers, fix them, and make sure accessibility is part of the process from the start. Whether you need an audit, hands-on fixes, training for your team, or a fully accessible website, I'm here to help you meet WCAG standards and create better experiences for all your users.</p>
