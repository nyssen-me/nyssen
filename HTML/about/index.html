Hola!

I’m Sergi Duran Nyssen, a wireless Web Designer & Developer. I work at Hillside Agency mainly for digital agency Sokvist, but I’m also available for freelance work.

What I do

I create an effective online presence for individuals, small-sized businesses and startups. I enjoy helping people translating ideas into design. My websites are designed according to W3C standards, cross browser compatible, user friendly and with latest SEO trends in mind.

Please visit my portfolio to see some of the works I've done.

What I use

Besides the usual tools for web design and web development, my main tools when working on a web project are: common sense, lots of patience, enthusiasm, open-mindedness and flexibility.

Things I like

Very personal things that I like and nice memories. I will be changing them from time to time, as my taste also changes with time.
