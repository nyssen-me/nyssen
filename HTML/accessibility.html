Accessibility Policy

In nyssen’s web site a series of measures have been adopted in order to improve the accessibility. The advantages that we get are, among other:

  • Facilitating the access of the persons, independently of their physical condition or their environment.
  • Allowing the access with different browsers and devices.
  • Clear and well structured contents.
  • Navigation improvement and user’s experience.

Adopted measures are:

  • Logical presentation order of the elements on the screen and of the contents in the code.
  • Simplified navigation system.
  • Use of the W3C standards: grammar HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Models of accessibility for the web page contents.
